The Gowanus Canal

Background

The Gowanus Canal is a 100-foot wide, 1.8-mile-long canal in Brooklyn. The residential neighborhoods of Gowanus, Park Slope, Cobble Hill, Carroll Gardens, and Red Hook surround the canal. The Gowanus Canal connects to Gowanus Bay and empties into the New York harbor.

The canal's waterfront is primarily commercial and industrial and is home to concrete plants, warehouses, and parking lots.
