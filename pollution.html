Possible Solutions to the Amazon Mess
(Mainly Just Ways to Avoid Scamazon)

I'm aware that there is no ethical consumption under capitalism, but we can all try to make the world a better place.

  • Cancel your Prime

    You get to save $120 per year. Plus, you don't need two day shipping for EVERYTHING.

  • Shop local

    If you need small items right away, go to your local bodega. You also get to support (small) local businesses.

  • Stop buying books on Amazon

    Instead, get books from independent and second-hand bookstores, or go to your local library.

  • Stop shopping at Whole Foods

    Just go to Food Bazaar. They're cheaper (and they're a minority-owned business too!).

  • Stop using Amazon Prime Video

    There are so many video streaming services out there. Use one of those.

  • Stop using Amazon Prime Music and Amazon Music Unlimited

    I've literally never heard of anyone using those services anyway.

  • Get rid of your Amazon Echo

    Alexa is always listening. Isn't that creepy?

  • Piracy

    Sad you can't watch that one Prime Video exclusive show or movie? Try to look for it somewhere else. *HINT HINT*
